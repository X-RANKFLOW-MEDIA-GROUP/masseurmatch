## BLOCO B: ADD-ONS (USD)

### 1. FEATURED PLACEMENT PER CITY
Pricing Model: Per city, monthly
Price: $19/mo per city
Rules and Limits: Maximum 5 featured slots per city at any time. If all 5 slots are filled, new purchases are queued for the next available slot. Each featured period lasts 30 days from activation. No stacking — one featured placement per profile per city at a time. Auto-renews unless cancelled.
UI Placement: City page (top carousel or top of list), Explore page (when filtering by that city). Badge: "Featured" on profile card.
Anti-Abuse: Only available to profiles with status "active" and identity verified. Profiles flagged or suspended lose featured status immediately with no refund for remaining days. 24-hour cooldown after cancellation before re-purchasing in the same city.

### 2. BOOST RANKING PER CITY
Pricing Model: Per city, one-time (7-day duration)
Price: $9 per boost
Rules and Limits: Each boost lasts exactly 7 days. Maximum 2 active boosts per profile across all cities. Cooldown: 3 days between boosts in the same city. Boost moves profile to top 20 percent of search results in the target city. Boost does not override Featured placement — Featured always ranks above Boosted.
UI Placement: Search results (boosted profiles appear higher, no visible badge). Dashboard shows active boosts with countdown.
Anti-Abuse: Maximum 4 boosts per month per profile. Cannot boost a profile that is currently featured in the same city (redundant). Only available to verified profiles.

### 3. AVAILABLE NOW TOGGLE
Pricing Model: Included in Starter, Pro, Elite. Add-on for Free plan: $5/mo.
Price: $5/mo (Free plan only)
Rules and Limits: Free plan add-on allows 3 activations per day, each lasting 4 hours max. Starter: 3 activations per day, 4 hours each. Pro and Elite: unlimited activations, 8 hours each. Toggle auto-expires after duration. Cannot be toggled on if profile is incomplete or unverified.
UI Placement: Explore page filter "Available Now". Profile card shows green "Available Now" indicator. Dashboard toggle with timer.
Anti-Abuse: If toggled on more than 16 hours in a 24-hour period (Pro/Elite), system auto-disables for 8 hours. Prevents "always on" abuse that degrades signal quality.

### 4. SPOTLIGHT CARD PER CITY
Pricing Model: Per city, one-time (48-hour duration)
Price: $14 per spotlight
Rules and Limits: Spotlight displays an expanded card (larger photo, bio preview, CTA) at the top of the city page for 48 hours. Maximum 2 spotlight slots per city at any time. If both slots are filled, purchase is queued. Maximum 2 spotlights per profile per month. 7-day cooldown in the same city after spotlight expires.
UI Placement: Top of city page, above Featured and regular listings. Clearly labeled "Spotlight" with distinct card design.
Anti-Abuse: Only available to profiles with 100 percent completion and identity verification. No spotlights during first 7 days of account creation (prevents spam signups).

### 5. PREMIUM CTA TRACKING
Pricing Model: Monthly
Price: $7/mo
Rules and Limits: Tracks which CTA buttons on the profile generate the most engagement (contact tap, phone reveal, message draft). Shows click counts, click sources (search, city page, direct link), and time-of-day patterns. Data retained for 90 days. Does not track client identity — only aggregate tap counts.
UI Placement: Dashboard analytics section. Adds "CTA Performance" tab.
Anti-Abuse: No abuse vector. Read-only analytics.

### 6. LEAD INSIGHTS PACK
Pricing Model: Monthly
Price: $12/mo
Rules and Limits: Shows anonymized lead data: how many unique visitors viewed the profile, which cities they searched from, which filters matched the profile, peak viewing times. Does not reveal any personal information about visitors. Data aggregated daily, retained for 90 days. Minimum 10 views required before data is shown (privacy threshold).
UI Placement: Dashboard analytics section. Adds "Lead Insights" tab with charts.
Anti-Abuse: Privacy threshold prevents identification of individual viewers. No export of raw data.

### 7. TRAVEL MODE UPGRADE
Pricing Model: Per trip, one-time
Price: $12 per trip
Rules and Limits: Free and Starter plans include limited travel destinations (0 and 1 respectively). This add-on unlocks 1 additional travel destination per purchase. Each trip must have specific dates (max 30-day trip). Auto-featured in destination city during travel dates (same visibility as a Featured placement). Travel badge ("Visiting") shown on profile card.
UI Placement: Dashboard travel section. Explore page shows "Visiting" badge on profile cards in destination city.
Anti-Abuse: Trip dates must be in the future and within 90 days. Cannot have more than 3 overlapping trips. Identity verification required.

### 8. EXPANDED SERVICE AREAS

## BLOCO D: "HIGHER PLAN COSTS LESS" MECHANISMS

### MECHANISM 1: Annual Pricing

Elite Annual: $69/mo equivalent ($828/yr)
Pro Monthly: $59/mo

Elite annual ($69/mo) is more expensive per month than Pro monthly ($59/mo), which is correct. However, the savings perception works because:

Elite monthly is $99/mo. The annual discount brings it to $69/mo (30% off).
Pro monthly is $59/mo. Pro annual is $47.17/mo (20% off).

The only scenario where Elite becomes "cheaper" relative to expectations is when comparing Elite Annual ($69/mo) to Elite Monthly ($99/mo) — a $30/mo savings. This is a standard annual commitment discount and does not violate pricing perception because the user is committing to 12 months.

No plan on monthly billing is ever cheaper than a lower-tier plan on the same billing cycle. This preserves pricing integrity.

### MECHANISM 2: Founder Deal (Limited to First 100)

Offer: First 100 Elite subscribers get Founder Pricing at $59/mo (locked for 12 months).
Effective Comparison: Founder Elite ($59/mo) equals Pro Monthly ($59/mo) — same price, higher tier.
Expiration: Founder pricing locks for 12 months from signup date. After 12 months, renews at standard Elite monthly ($99/mo) or annual ($69/mo) at user's choice.
Visibility: "Founder" badge on profile during the locked period. Counter shows "X of 100 spots remaining."

Why this does not break perception:
1. It is explicitly limited (100 spots) and time-bound (12 months), so it reads as a launch incentive, not a permanent price.
2. The Founder price equals Pro monthly — it does not undercut Pro. It gives Pro-priced access to Elite features as an early adopter reward.
3. After 12 months, the price normalizes. Users who got the deal feel rewarded. Users who did not feel urgency to act on future promotions.

## BLOCO E: UPSELL FLOW (8 STEPS)

### STEP 1: TRIGGER — HIGH VIEWS, LOW CONTACT TAPS
When: Profile has 30+ views in 7 days but fewer than 2 contact taps.
Action: Show in-dashboard notification: "Your profile is getting attention but visitors are not reaching out. Here is how to convert views into inquiries."
Recommended: Profile Refresh ($5) + Available Now add-on ($5/mo) if on Free plan.

### STEP 2: TRIGGER — CITY WITH HIGH DEMAND
When: The user's city has 20+ searches in the last 7 days and the user does not have Featured or Boost active.
Action: Show banner on dashboard: "[City] is trending. [X] clients searched for therapists this week."
Recommended: Boost ($9 one-time) or Featured Placement ($19/mo).

### STEP 3: TRIGGER — PROFILE INCOMPLETE
When: Profile is missing bio, photos, or verification after 72 hours.
Action: Show progress bar on dashboard with specific missing items.
Recommended: Complete profile first (no upsell — completion is prerequisite for paid features).

### STEP 4: TRIGGER — SECOND CITY INTEREST
When: User views the Travel section or searches for therapists in another city.
Action: Show modal: "Want to appear in [City]? Add it as a service area or travel destination."
Recommended: Expanded Service Area ($6/mo) or Travel Mode Upgrade ($12/trip). If user is on Free, recommend Starter upgrade first.

### STEP 5: TRIGGER — PHOTO LIMIT REACHED
When: User attempts to upload a photo but has reached their plan's limit.
Action: Show upgrade prompt: "You have reached your photo limit. Unlock 2 more slots or upgrade your plan."
Recommended: Extra Photos Pack ($8 one-time) or plan upgrade.

### STEP 6: TRIGGER — 7 DAYS INTO TRIAL
When: User is 7 days into their 14-day trial.
Action: Show in-dashboard card: "You are halfway through your trial. Here is what you have used so far: [feature usage summary]."
Recommended: Continue to paid (no add-on push during trial — focus on conversion).

### STEP 7: GROWTH STACK BUNDLE
When: User has been on a paid plan for 30+ days and has not purchased any add-ons.
Action: Show one-time offer in dashboard: "Growth Stack — everything you need to maximize visibility."
Bundle Contents: Featured Placement (1 city) + 2 Boosts + Premium CTA Tracking + Profile Refresh.
Bundle Price: $39/mo (individual value: $19 + $18 + $7 + $5 = $49/mo — 20% savings).
Presentation: Side-by-side comparison showing individual vs bundle pricing.

### STEP 8: TRIGGER — PLAN DOWNGRADE INTENT
When: User clicks "Cancel" or "Downgrade" on their subscription page.
Action: Show retention modal: "Before you go — here is what you will lose: [list of plan-specific features with usage data]. Would you like to switch to [lower plan] instead of cancelling?"
Recommended: Offer one-month discount (20% off current plan) or suggest downgrade to next tier instead of full cancellation.

## BLOCO F: POLICIES AND LOGS

### F1. MINIMUM LOGS FOR ADD-ON ROI MEASUREMENT

Required event logs (stored in analytics table):

1. add_on_purchased — add-on name, price, user_id, timestamp, city (if applicable)
2. add_on_activated — add-on name, activation timestamp, expiration timestamp, city
3. add_on_expired — add-on name, expiration timestamp, was_renewed (boolean)
4. add_on_cancelled — add-on name, cancellation timestamp, reason (if provided)
5. boost_impression — profile_id, city, timestamp (each time boosted profile appears in search)
6. featured_impression — profile_id, city, timestamp (each time featured profile appears)
7. spotlight_impression — profile_id, city, timestamp
8. available_now_toggled — profile_id, toggled_on/off, timestamp, duration
9. contact_tap — profile_id, source (search/city_page/direct/smart_match), timestamp, viewer_city
10. profile_view — profile_id, source, timestamp, viewer_city
11. smart_match_appearance — profile_id, match_rank, timestamp
12. category_filter_impression — profile_id, category, timestamp

Each log entry must include: event_name, profile_id, timestamp, metadata (JSONB).

### F2. RETENTION RECOMMENDATIONS

Log Retention Periods:
  Raw event logs: 12 months (then aggregate and archive)
  Aggregated daily metrics: 24 months
  Billing and transaction logs: 7 years (legal/tax compliance)
  Add-on purchase history: Permanent (tied to user account)
  Moderation and safety logs: 3 years

Data Aggregation Schedule:
  Daily: Aggregate raw events into daily counts per profile per city.
  Weekly: Generate weekly summary per profile (views, taps, boost impressions).
  Monthly: Generate monthly report data for City Demand Digest and Growth Digest emails.

### F3. RANKING FAIRNESS RULES

When Featured and Available Now are active simultaneously, the ranking algorithm applies this priority stack:

TIER 1 — SPOTLIGHT (highest visibility):
  Spotlight cards always appear at the absolute top of city page listings.
  Maximum 2 per city. First-come ordering. No algorithmic interference.

TIER 2 — FEATURED + AVAILABLE NOW:
  Profiles that are both Featured and Available Now appear next.
  Within this tier, sort by recency of Available Now activation (most recent first).

TIER 3 — FEATURED ONLY:
  Featured profiles without Available Now active.
  Within this tier, sort by profile completeness score, then recency of last update.

TIER 4 — AVAILABLE NOW ONLY (NON-FEATURED):
  Profiles with Available Now active but no Featured placement.
  Elite plans with priority badge appear above Pro and Starter in this tier.
  Within same plan level, sort by recency of toggle activation.

TIER 5 — BOOSTED:
  Boosted profiles without Featured or Available Now.
  Sort by boost start date (most recent first).

TIER 6 — STANDARD (ORGANIC):
  All other active, verified profiles.
  Sort by: plan tier (Elite > Pro > Starter > Free), then profile completeness, then recency of last update.

ANTI-STACKING RULES:
1. A profile cannot hold Spotlight + Featured + Boost in the same city simultaneously. Maximum 2 visibility add-ons per profile per city.
2. If a profile is Featured in a city and purchases a Spotlight in the same city, the Featured benefit is paused (not charged) during the Spotlight period.
3. Boosted profiles cannot appear above Featured profiles regardless of boost count.
4. Available Now does not stack with itself — toggling off and on does not reset position.
5. Profiles with active content flags or pending moderation are excluded from all premium placements until resolved.

TRANSPARENCY:
1. No "shadow" ranking. Every profile in a paid tier sees their current rank position in the dashboard.
2. Featured and Spotlight placements are labeled in the UI so clients know it is a paid placement.
3. Available Now indicator is factual (time-based) and auto-expires — it cannot be faked.
