# Search Interest Index

This note explains how to read the hourly search interest values used for MasseurMatch SEO and conversion decisions.

## Plain meaning

The values are relative demand scores, not exact visitor counts.

- 44 means normal movement. There is real interest, but it is not a peak.
- 48 means the market is getting slightly warmer than normal.
- 81 means strong demand. This is a high intent window and should be treated as a priority conversion period.

Higher number means more people are searching at that moment compared with the strongest point in the selected data period.

## Decision rules

Use these thresholds for product, SEO, and ranking logic:

- 0 to 39: low demand. Keep standard ranking and standard calls to action.
- 40 to 60: normal demand. Keep conversion elements visible and monitor click activity.
- 61 to 79: strong demand. Promote available therapists and prioritize direct contact actions.
- 80 to 100: peak demand. Activate peak hour ranking, stronger available now messaging, and premium visibility logic.

## MasseurMatch implementation guidance

When demand is 80 or higher:

1. Push therapists marked as available now higher in the results.
2. Show stronger copy such as "Available now in your area".
3. Prioritize direct contact buttons for call, text, WhatsApp, and email.
4. Track profile views and contact clicks separately for this window.
5. Use the data for therapist upsells such as peak visibility boosts.

## Important limitation

These numbers do not mean 44, 48, or 81 people searched. They are normalized index values. The safest way to use them is to compare demand intensity by hour, then validate the pattern with real MasseurMatch click and contact data.
